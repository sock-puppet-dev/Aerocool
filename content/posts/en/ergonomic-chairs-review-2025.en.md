## Why Choosing the Right Chair Matters

Spending long hours in a seated position is the daily routine for millions. However, the wrong chair can lead to:
- back and neck pain  
- poor posture  
- chronic fatigue  
- reduced productivity  

That’s why selecting an ergonomic chair is an investment in both your health and performance.

## 🧠 Expert Buying Tips

1. **Look for adjustable lumbar support** — It's a must.
2. **Armrests with 3D or 4D adjustments** — For better wrist and shoulder alignment.
3. **Mesh or fabric** — More breathable than leather.
4. **Wheels with lock function** — Essential for wooden floors.
5. **Always test in person if possible** — Everyone’s spine is unique.
